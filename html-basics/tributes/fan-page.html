Biography

Gabby is an Instagram famous socialite who descended from the landed gentry
She's that classic girl who's had everything you could ever buy, but never had what
she probably needs... which love from her father, who's been absent her entire life.


Beneath her poise and polished exterior is a young woman who surrounds herself
with people who don't really have her best interests at heart; she has deep-seated
issues ranging from substance addiction to anger management. Though the
behavior feels all about her trying to be noticed and loved. And she surrounds
herself with people who don't really have her best interests at heart.

She's fascinating. She's hilarious. She's a great one for a cause but without really
understanding the cause. She's just gets behind something and hilariously gets it
wrong all the time. She's always saying the wrong things without really knowing
what she's saying, which adds to her hilarity and charm.

Her time with the outlaws turns out to be life-defining for her.
